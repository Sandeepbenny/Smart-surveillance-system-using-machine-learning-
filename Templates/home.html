SMART SURVELLIENCE SYSTEM USING MACHINE LEARNING

Abstract

People only want one thing these days: to feel secure and safe. CCTV is the most widely utilized security technology (closed circuit Television). The price to install CCTV varies according to the system's size and intended purpose. It is typically implemented at hospitals, shopping centers, parking lots, etc. Yet, with the use of CCTV, one can keep an eye on the region round-the-clock, or, if the film is saved somewhere, it may be retrieved when needed. Although it can be used to prevent crime and enable law enforcement to locate and solve a crime, it is unable to identify or detect the person who is involved. The need for protecting properties has made providing security one of the most crucial duties. The video surveillance system was thus established to ensure security. The conduct, activities, or other information of people in a particular area is monitored using a video surveillance system. The use of video surveillance has now been expanded across other industries rather than remaining restricted to providing localized security. Intelligent CCTV Application This project was developed utilizing the most recent programming language and the rapidly developing discipline of computer science known as "Computer Vision." It is a python GUI application that can operate on any operating system, uses a webcam, and contains a variety of capabilities that are not in typical CCTV. It indicates that this project enables computer vision or, to put it another way, allows the computer to watch. A Normal standard CCTV system can just take pictures.
